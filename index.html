Cuisine

A delicious plate of creamy fettuccine alfredo pasta, this plate was created in Rome around the year 1914, by restaurateur Alfredo di Lelio. It was originally a few ingredients fettuccine with butter and parmesan cheese mixed to help his pregnant wife's missing appetite. Since then it has gained international popularity within America, flourishing and becoming the favourite dish to order on the menu across resturants world-wide today.

Art

The Mona Lisa is a very iconic piece of art that is known throughout the world, painted by the famous Leonardo Da Vinci in 1503-1506. Although, that is the known year, Leo may have been working on the painting for an extra 10 years longer than estimated. Later in 1519 King Francis I, acquired the painting after Leonardo's death in may of that year. Thus making it now the property of the French Republic.
